Voting Roadmap

Phase-by-phase breakdown of the election lifecycle

1. Voter Registration

The first step is ensuring you're on the electoral roll. Check your eligibility and submit your application before the deadline.

2. Candidate Research

Know who you're voting for. Review manifestos, track records, and party platforms to make an informed choice.

3. Find Polling Station

Locate your assigned polling booth. Verify the timing and ensure you have transportation planned for election day.

4. Casting Your Vote

On election day, bring your valid ID. Follow the instructions at the booth and cast your ballot securely.

5. Result Declaration

After polls close, counting begins. Results are verified and announced through official channels.
